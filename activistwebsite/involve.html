Getting Involved!!


anyone can help by becoming an advocate for mental health awarness; and there are many ways to volunteer your time to mental health including...


Making some noise

- Join/create a dosomething.org campaign on mental wellness

- Share your story with mental health

- Hand out green ribbons for people to wear/hang


Helping others that are struggling

- Volunteer with Crisis Text Line

- Volunteer with the National Suicide Prevention Lifeline

- Fundraise


Be there for others

- Check in on friends/family


Share Resources

- Follow mental-health based accounts; repost their content

- Share infographics on mental wellness
